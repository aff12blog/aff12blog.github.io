It's Ash Wednesday! So begins the 40 days of Lent. Since today is an obligatory day of fasting and abstinence for Catholics I thought I would share one of our favorite Lenten recipes.

Ravioli Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups tomato/pasta sauce
  • 2 bags (32 oz) frozen cheese ravioli
  • 3 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:

  1. Spread ½ cup sauce into greased 11 X 7 baking dish.
  2. Layer with ravioli (no need to thaw), 1 ½ spinach, ½ cup mozzarella cheese and 1 cup of pasta sauce.
  3. Repeat layers.
  4. Sprinkle top with ½ cup mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until edges are bubbly and cheese is melted.
  6. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
