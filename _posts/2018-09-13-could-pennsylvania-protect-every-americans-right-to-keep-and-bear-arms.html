According to Fox News senior judicial analyst Judge Andrew Napolitano, maybe so.  I recommend reading this article from The Tenth Amendment Center blog,

Pennsylvania Bill Would Set Foundation to Build a Gun Rights “Sanctuary State”

The article reports that the Right to Bear
Arms Protection Act, House Bill 357(HB357) , was introduced by Rep. Daryl Metcalfe (R- Cranberry Township)on Sept. 5, with 41 bipartisan cosponsors.  The bill would render any Federal law which attempts to register, restrict or ban a firearm, or to limit the size of a magazine of a firearm, “unenforceable within the borders of this Commonwealth.” This restriction would apply to both federal and state agents.

Judge Napolitano noted that a single state taking this step would make federal gun laws “nearly impossible” to enforce.

HB357 was referred to the House Judiciary Committee where it must pass by a majority vote before moving forward in the legislative process.
